About Me

My name is Fran, I am full stack developer trained in Ironhack. My family is galician, a northwestern region of Spain, but was actually born in Mexico. You see... my family is one of immigrants. For four generations my family has moved to a different country. I have lived in Mexico, Venezuela, United States, Sweden and currently live in Spain. In Madrid more specifically. I studied Journalism at Complutense University, but after working for a few years at Thomson Reuters I created my own business: Star Wash. It is a premium car wash located in the south of the spanish capital. I opened the business in 2017 and it is still working, in spite of many struggles. Surviving a global pandemic and it's aftermath has been the hardest challenge that I have encontoured. After I was sure that the business would be ok, and after realizing I was completely burnt out, I embarked in a new adventure: software development! I begun my studies at Ironhack's web development bootcamp. An intense 9 week program that completely changed the direction of my profesional career.
