About Me

My name is Laia, and I have a strong passion for design and innovation. With a solid background in fashion design and some years of experience in the industry, I decided to pivot my career and explore the exciting world of web development. Through my previous roles managing online retail platforms, including my own womenswear fashion label and a high-end vintage boutique, I've gained a profound understanding of the digital landscape and honed my ability to craft compelling and user-friendly online experiences. My fascination with aesthetics and my unwavering commitment to delivering excellence are driving forces behind my ambition to continuously learn and evolve as a web developer.
